FAQ:

  • How does this work?

    OP Replay automatically checks accounts to see if they are in game and then requests a recording from op.gg

  • How can I watch a replay?

    Head over to op.gg and search for the account you want to spectate. If you check match history there should be a replay button for each match. Download the replay batch file and run.

  • Don't other programs that do this?

    Unlike different replay programs, OP Replay automatically requests recordings of your games for you, without the need to download any software or register on the site. The only thing you ever have to download is the replay itself from op.gg if you want to watch it. However, there are plenty of other replay services out there, and you should use the one that works best for you.
